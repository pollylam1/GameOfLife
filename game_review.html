Welcome

Here's the Rule of Game of life.

First, there are some random box on the table already.
• If a box has life and it has less than 2 neighbors. It dies of loneliness.
• If a box has life and it has more than 3 neighbors. It dies of overpopulation.
• If a box has life and it has 2 to 3 neighbors. Nothing changes for this box.
• If a box has no life and it has 3 neighbors, the box in next generation fills with life because of reproduction.

＊ You may change the rules.
＊ You may reset the table, change the color by pen, color mode and pattern of new life.
＊ There will be color change when the life in different status, try to find out how it's change!
